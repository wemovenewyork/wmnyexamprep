    Common questions
    Do you charge a monthly subscription?
    No. Every plan is a one-time payment. There are no automatic renewals, no recurring charges, and no card-on-file after your purchase.
    Are you affiliated with any NYC government agency?
    No. WMNY Exam Prep is an independent study service operated by We Move New York. We have no affiliation with DCAS, the City of New York, or any government agency. Agency names and exam titles are used for descriptive identification only.
    How is this different from JobTestPrep, Mometrix, or PracticeTestGeeks?
    We're NYC-specific, built by people who actually went through these processes as transit workers. Our content is tailored to the real exams — not generic civil service templates recycled across 50 states. And we charge once, not monthly.
    Do you offer refunds?
    Yes. We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. Contact us within 7 days of purchase and we'll issue a full refund, no questions asked.
    How often is the question bank updated?
    We monitor official Notices of Examination as they're published by the issuing agencies. When an exam notice changes — new format, new content areas, updated requirements — we update the corresponding module within days.
    Can I switch which agency module I study?
    All-Access Pass and Coach-Up Bundle holders have access to all 6 modules. Single Agency purchasers are locked to one module, but can contact support to request a switch — we'll review case by case.
